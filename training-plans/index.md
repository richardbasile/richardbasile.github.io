# Training Plans

Following is the two-plan sequence I am following to prepare for both a half and a full marathon. The half marathon plan establishes the base: aerobic fitness, lifting fundamentals, and the habit of running four days a week. The marathon plan picks up immediately after race day and builds on that foundation toward 26.2 miles. Both plans integrate running and strength training, follow a three-week build/build/deload cycle, and include detailed day-by-day workouts.

---

## [Half Marathon](half-marathon.html)

**16 weeks · 4 running days · 2 strength days**

A beginner half marathon plan that builds from a 4-mile long run to a 12-mile peak over three 3-week build/deload cycles, with a two-week taper. Running days are Monday (easy), Wednesday (mid), Thursday (quality), and Saturday (long). Strength training runs Tuesday (heavy compounds + Olympic lifts with a cycling warmup) and Friday (bodyweight). Quality work begins in Week 4 and progresses from time-based intervals to tempo and half marathon pace efforts. Week 12 includes a 10K tune-up race on Sunday, with Friday becoming a rest day and the Saturday cell split to show the pre-race shakeout and Sunday race. Week 13 drops strength entirely and Monday becomes a rest day to allow proper recovery before resuming peak training. Peak long run is 12 miles in Week 14.

## [Marathon](marathon.html)

**15 weeks · 4 running days · 2 strength days**

A follow-up marathon plan that picks up immediately after the half marathon. Early weeks are compressed relative to a typical plan to account for the half marathon fitness base: long runs start at 10 miles in Week 2 and reach 12 by Week 3. Weekly mileage builds through two-a-days on Monday (capped at 5 miles total) and Wednesday (PM run up to 7 miles). Long runs step up by no more than 2 miles per build week, reaching 18 miles in Week 11 before the peak 20-miler in Week 12. Thursday shifts from interval work to sustained marathon pace running, building from 2 miles at goal pace to 5 miles at peak. Strength training scales to maintenance from Week 7 and winds down to a symbolic final session in race week.
