drink
Raspberry (fresh/frozen)
Blend and strain — pulp and seeds clog soft flask valves. Pairs well with citric acid.
Avoid pulp in soft flasks (clogs bite valves). Flavor fades when cold and amplifies when warm — taste-test at run temperature. Sweetness perception drops during hard efforts, so slightly over-flavoring is not a bad idea.
