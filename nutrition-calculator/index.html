At 80–90 g/hr, sweetness can suppress drinking late in a long effort. Consider the maltodextrin/fructose formula for better palatability at high concentrations.
The all-in-one drink combines carbohydrate and electrolytes in a single bottle with no separate gel needed. Target ~6% carbohydrate concentration (60 g per liter). Above that, GI distress risk rises and palatability drops. Carry enough volume to drink 500–1,000 ml per hour. Use a soft flask or hydration vest for runs beyond 90 minutes.
